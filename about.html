What the #%!$ is this?

A few amusing conversations later, we started tracking how many US capitals we've urinated at and the idea for a website began!

What counts?

Any time you pee in the city limits of a US state capital, it counts! Whether it's in a public restroom, a bush, or a bottle in the car, it counts!
